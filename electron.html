How it works

Having a cloud sound engine allows to have multiple clients and remote management.

Thanks to that you can install software on how many devices you want: smartphones, tablets, laptops, and while their in broadcasting mode - you can manage their sound sources through a web-browser.

So you can just send journalist with "one-button-app" to the field and have sound engineer elswhere to manage their broadcast remotely.
