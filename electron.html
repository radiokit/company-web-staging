How it works

Having a cloud sound engine allows for multiple clients and remote management.

Thanks to that you can install software on as many devices as you want: smartphones, tablets, laptops, and while they are in broadcasting mode - you can manage their sound sources through a web-browser.

So you can just send journalist with "one-button-app" to the field and have sound engineer elswhere to manage their broadcast remotely.
